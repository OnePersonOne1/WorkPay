## 🇺🇸 English (en-US)

### App name (max 30 chars)
```
WorkPay: Shift & Wage Calc
```

### Short description (max 80 chars)
```
Ad-free offline work schedule & wage calculator for part-time and student jobs.
```

### Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
WorkPay is an ad-free, offline work schedule and wage calculator built for part-time and work-study workers.

Log your shifts on the calendar and WorkPay automatically calculates your daily, weekly, and monthly pay from your hourly wage. No sign-up, no ads, no data collection. Everything stays on your device.

■ Who it's for
· Hourly part-time workers
· Work-study students
· Anyone juggling multiple jobs and unsure of their monthly pay
· Anyone who wants a clean app with no ads or tracking

■ Key features
· Calendar-based shift scheduling (color-coded by workplace)
· Automatic wage calculation — show daily / weekly / monthly pay
· Multiple workplaces with combined totals
· Bulk-add recurring shifts
· Undo / redo
· Backup / restore (saved as a file on your device)
· Light / dark theme, English / Korean

■ Korea labor-law options (toggleable)
· Weekly holiday allowance, night premium, daily/weekly overtime
· Holiday-work premium
· 3.3% business income & social insurance deductions
· Fine-grained constants you can tune yourself (expert settings)

■ Privacy
· No ads · no tracking · no data ever sent over the internet
· All data stays on your device
· No account or login required

The displayed currency unit (원, $, USD, etc.) can be changed freely in settings — it is only a display label and does not affect any calculation.

Feedback is always welcome.
```
